If you are a creative one and loves writing, then content writing is the right skill set for you. It involves writing tasks. Content writing is also a domain and has huge fields related to writing content. Some of them are the following:

1- Articles and Blog writing:

  This field involves writing articles for the website. In this field, you will write articles for a website which are usually known as Blog sites.


2- Translation:

  This field involves tasks related to translation. In this field, you will translate content from one language to another language.


3- Proof Reading & Editing:

  This field involves tasks related to proofreading. In this field, you will proofread and edit the articles already written by the client.


4- Resume Writing:

  In this field, you will write resumes for your clients.


5- Book writing:

  This field involves tasks related to book writing. In this field, you will write a book related to a specific niche for your client.


6- Ghostwriting:

  This field involves tasks related to writing. In this field, you will write content but you will not use your name.


7- legal writing:

  This field involves tasks related to writing legal content


8- Scriptwriting:

  This field involves tasks related to writing scripts for videos or podcasts.


9- Product Description writing:

  This field involves tasks related to writing product description.
